Questions answered

Frequently asked questions

How does GolfTripAI work? ▾
GolfTripAI analyses your preferences — budget, handicap, travel dates, group size and desired trip style — and generates a fully personalised golf holiday itinerary. It draws on extensive knowledge of golf destinations and resorts popular with UK travellers to provide recommendations that are genuinely useful and realistic.
Do I book my trip through GolfTripAI? ▾
Not at present. GolfTripAI is a planning and recommendation tool. We provide expert recommendations for destinations, courses, hotels and activities — you then book directly with the relevant providers. We're working on direct booking integrations for a future release.
How accurate are the recommendations? ▾
Recommendations are tailored to your specific budget, handicap and preferences, drawing on extensive knowledge of golf destinations popular with UK travellers. We recommend verifying specific pricing and availability directly with providers before booking.
Can I generate multiple trips? ▾
Free accounts include one trip generation. Our Premium plan (£9.99/month or £49/year) offers unlimited trip generations — ideal if you're planning multiple trips, comparing destinations or organising for a golf society.
