Questions answered

Frequently asked questions

What's included in the three trip options? ▾
Every plan generates three versions of your golf holiday — Budget (great value, quality courses at lower cost), Mid-Range (the sweet spot most golfers choose) and Luxury (premium courses, top resorts). Each includes course recommendations with technical data, hotels, restaurants, bars, a 5-day itinerary and estimated costs.
Can people in my group travel from different airports? ▾
Yes — you can add multiple departure airports. GolfTrip finds destinations with good connections from all of them, so everyone in the group can get there without one person travelling for 6 hours.
Do I book through GolfTrip? ▾
GolfTrip is a planning tool. We provide recommendations and links to trusted booking partners — Skyscanner for flights, Booking.com and Vrbo for accommodation, GolfNow for tee times. You complete your booking directly with them.
What's the difference between the plans? ▾
The Free plan gives you one full trip comparison to try the product. Pro unlocks unlimited planning, saved trips and seasonal recommendations. Society is built for groups — shared planning tools, voting on courses and exportable trip packs.
